What is Employee Voices?

A simple way to share stories about working here — if and when you want to. Browse categories, pick a topic that resonates, and share in your own words.

What's optional?

Everything. Browsing, sharing, and tracking for recognition are all your choice — every single time.

How tracking works

If you choose a personal link for recognition, we track clicks on that specific link. That's it. If you share without a link, nothing is tracked individually.

What we do NOT do

🚫 We do not monitor your social media accounts
🚫 We do not auto-post on your behalf
🚫 We do not track you without your explicit consent
🚫 We do not share individual data with managers
🚫 We do not penalize anyone for skipping or opting out

Your data, your terms

Recognition tracking is opt-in per interaction. You can choose differently every time. Individual reporting is visible only to 1–2 program owners and is used solely for optional recognition and celebration.

Questions? Reach out to your Talent & Culture team.
